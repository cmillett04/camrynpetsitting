About me!

Loving, Caring, and Responsible!

Hi! My name is Camryn, and I am a responsible and energetic pet lover. I have taken care of 2 dogs of my own throughout my life (Ralph- a shar pei and pug mix and Leonard- a black lab puppy), as well as my bunny and chickens. I have been a pet sitter / dog walker for over a year on Rover, but before that I had also taken care of the pets of many friends and family members while they were away. I will care for pets of all different types, ages, and sizes! I love spending time with animals and would love to give your pet the care and attention they need while you are away. Whatever preferences you have for your pet, I am happy to care for your pet in the same way you do. My experience with and love for animals make me a great candidate for pet-sitting or walking. Hoping to meet and care for your pet soon!

Generally, on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Friday, I am available anytime before 9:00am or after 5pm. On Tuesdays and Thursdays, I am available after 3pm, and anytime on the weekends. However, my weekday schedule can be relatively flexible for things like drop-in visits and walks, so please feel free to reach out and ask about the particular times/dates you need. I am also available to stay overnight with your pets if needed.

As a college student, I unfortunately do not have the ability to pet sit in my own apartment. However, I am available to house and pet sit at your house or apartment during the day or overnight and make visits as needed.
